🥣 Oatmeal

Local meeting recorder. Audio is transcribed by Whisper on this machine and never leaves it. Your coding agent turns transcripts into notes.
1 · Record Title your meeting, hit Record. First run downloads the Whisper model once (~80 MB), then it's offline.
2 · Share audio Pick Entire screen and CHECK "share system audio" (or the meeting tab + tab audio). A window share has no audio.
3 · Get notes After the meeting, tell your coding agent: "write up my meeting". It reads the transcript and commits notes.
